## Chosen Community

r/TrueFilm on reddit community
r/TrueFilm is a community about in-depth discussions on films. People share their opinions on movies, their critiques, ask for advice on what to watch, and just conversate. 

## Labels

1. Critique - a structured argument about something, with specific evidence provided
2. Opinion - a clear stance without supporting arguments
3. Reaction - immediate emotional response, with or without supporting argments. The author isn't trying to argue a specific point, but rather sharing their emotions.
